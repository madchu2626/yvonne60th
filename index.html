Resort FAQ

More info at flamingoresort.com

What time is check-in & check-out?

Check-in is at 4 pm and check-out is at 12 pm. A $100 nightly hold is placed on credit cards for incidentals. Early check-in/late checkout available upon request at 707-545-8530.

What is in the rooms?

Flat-screen TV, air conditioning/heater, mini refrigerator, complimentary coffee/tea service, hair dryer, iron and ironing board, and voicemail access.

Is the resort pet-friendly?

Dogs are the only pets permitted. Fee: $50/dog daily, maximum two dogs per room.

Do you accept cash?

The Flamingo is a cashless property and does not accept cash for payments. Cash gratuities are welcome.

Where is the nearest airport?

Santa Rosa Airport (STS) is under 15 minutes away. San Francisco (SFO), Oakland (OAK), and Sacramento (SMF) also serve the area.

What is the smoking/vaping policy?

The Flamingo is a non-smoking property. Violations incur a $250 cleaning fee. Cannabis smoking restricted to designated areas only.

What are the pool hours?

Central pool operates 7:00 am to 10:00 pm daily. Separate lap swimming pool available by reservation.

Is there a restaurant on-site?

Flamingo Lazeaway Club serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Poolside mobile ordering is also available.

What are the resort quiet hours?

Quiet hours are 10 pm – 7 am every day.

Is there a fee for extra adults in a room?

Additional guests are charged $20 per night, plus tax. Children under 12 stay free with existing bedding.

Do you offer rollaway beds?

Limited availability at $35/night, first come first served. Contact Guest Services at 707-545-8530 to reserve.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours prior to arrival to avoid a one night's room rate penalty.

What are the taxes and fees?

Daily Resort Fee: $35/room plus tax. Occupancy Tax: 11%, County Tourism Tax: 2%, CA Tourism Tax: 0.195%, Santa Rosa BZ Assessment: 3%.

Is WiFi included?

Yes, high-speed WiFi is included in the resort fee and available property-wide.
